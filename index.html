Policy & Industry Drivers

What's moving the sector
🏭
PLI Scheme — Solar PV

₹24,000 Cr allocated for domestic solar cell and module manufacturing. Direct beneficiaries: Waaree Energies, Premier Energies, Tata Power Solar. Designed to reduce China import dependency (India currently imports ~85% of solar cells from China). Target: 50 GW/year domestic capacity by 2027.

⚗️
National Green Hydrogen Mission

₹600 Cr budget FY26. SIGHT scheme for electrolyzer manufacturing: Waaree (300 MW), Adani Enterprises (101.5 MW) won bids. Production-linked incentive for green hydrogen production targets 5 MMT/year by 2030. 2027–2035 is the real revenue window — invest now for future payoff.

⚡
FDRE / Storage Mandates

Ministry of Power mandated FDRE (Firm & Dispatchable RE) tenders — hybrid solar + wind + BESS. ACME Solar and JSW Energy are frontrunners. FDRE tariffs (₹4.50–5.50/kWh) are 60–100% premium over plain solar. 13.57 GW BESS pipeline under development nationally.

🌐
Import Tariff Protection

40% Basic Customs Duty on solar modules + 25% on cells from China. Protects PLI manufacturers domestically. However: US imposed preliminary anti-dumping duties on Indian solar modules — creates short-term headwind for exporters like Waaree (US facility partially mitigates this).

📋
PPA Signing Reform

MoP mandated PPA signing within 30 days of LOA issuance — addressing 40+ GW projects stuck in PPA limbo. This directly unlocks capex deployment timelines for Adani Green, NTPC Green, JSW Energy and ACME Solar. Expect accelerated commissioning in FY27.

🔋
PM-KUSUM + Rooftop Solar

PM Surya Ghar: free electricity for 1 crore households. ₹75,000 Cr allocated. Primary beneficiary: Tata Power Solar (rooftop segment). Creates a massive distributed solar demand that is completely distinct from utility-scale — a true second growth engine for the right companies.

Final Verdict

🏆
Most Trustworthy Long-Term Bet
NTPC Green Energy + Tata Power

NTPC for pure capital safety — sovereign backing, AAA rating, 100% PPA, zero controversy, 60 GW pipeline. Tata Power for safety with growth — the only full-stack operator, Tata governance premium, Mundra overhang is finite and declining. Both are institutional-grade investments.

Low volatility • High conviction • Multi-decade compounders
🚀
Highest Upside Potential
Waaree Energies

Debt-free with ₹5,393 Cr net cash. Q3 FY26 EBITDA +167% YoY. 24 GW order book = 2 years visibility. 22.3 GW module + 5.4 GW cell capacity. PLI + SIGHT winner. US facility. Backward integrating to wafers. At the right price, this is a once-in-a-decade manufacturing story for India.

Explosive growth • Debt-free • Multiple catalysts
⚠️
Most Overrated / Highest Risk
Adani Green Energy

The underlying assets are real. PPA quality is genuinely excellent. But at ₹1.41 lakh crore market cap, you're paying a steep premium for a company with low interest coverage, rising debt (₹30,000 Cr capex ahead), US federal indictment of its chairman, and TotalEnergies halting new investments. The risk-reward does not justify the valuation.

Strong assets • Poor risk-reward at current price
